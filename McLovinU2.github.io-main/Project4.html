Project 4: Helping families in there estate planning

Upon the unfortunate passing of a family member, a series of intricate inheritance legal proceedings unfolds. To empower our clients with greater insight and enable them to make informed decisions, I collaborated with the estate planning department to create an Estate Planning Dashboard.

This dashboard serves as a valuable tool for clarifying the implications of a family member's passing. It elucidates various legal responsibilities associated with inheritance. Among these, the most crucial concerns for family members are the inheritance tax and the available interest rate options.

The EPX diagram

The EPX represents the cumulative inheritance tax for two instances of inheritance. In the first instance, each specific tax rate mandates a fixed amount due. In the second instance, the time factor comes into play with the previously selected rate. Over time, a particular rate becomes more advantageous if the surviving partner's tenure extends.
